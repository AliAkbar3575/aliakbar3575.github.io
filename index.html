About Me

I am an AI/ML researcher with hands-on experience building RAG pipelines, LLM-powered agents, and multimodal AI systems using LangChain, LangGraph, LangSmith, PyTorch, and vector databases. I completed my B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the University of Dhaka and worked as a Research Assistant at the Data & Design Lab (CSE, University of Dhaka), contributing to a government-funded ML deployment project, an LLM-powered domain chatbot, and a real-time data visualization dashboard. My research spans Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and Healthcare AI across 4 published and ongoing works — including microbacterial instance segmentation, gut microbiome-based IBS diagnosis, and plant leaf disease VQA — with an acknowledged contribution on an ACL Findings 2025 paper. I was Runner-up (Top 2) at the ACM UbiComp 2021 International Nurse Care Activity Recognition Challenge. I am currently seeking opportunities in AI/ML Engineering roles at the intersection of Agentic AI, Multimodal Systems, and Healthcare AI.

Applied ML Projects & Research

Nurse Care Activity Recognition from Accelerometer Data

Engineered time-frequency features from raw accelerometer signals using FFT and DWT. Applied Variance Threshold, ANOVA, and Chi-square feature selection. Achieved 87% accuracy using LightGBM — contributed to runner-up at ACM UbiComp 2021 Challenge.

MLFFT/DWTSignal Processing
GitHub

Micro-bacterial Segmentation using ViT-based Hybrid Model

Max-ViT backbone-based Mask R-CNN for end-to-end microbacterial instance segmentation on M-ROSE dataset. Outperformed ResNet50 Mask R-CNN, Mask2Former, and YOLOv11 — mAP 79.31%, F1 70.01%. Applied ROI Activation CAM for interpretability.

PyTorchViTMask R-CNNOpenCV
GitHub

Gut Microbiome ML for IBS Diagnosis

Curated hybrid dataset of 1,109 gut microbiome samples (NCBI + Bangladeshi samples). Processed through CZID pipeline, optimized with Optuna Bayesian optimization under 5-fold CV. Logistic Regression achieved 79.73% accuracy, MCC 0.5876. Manuscript in preparation.

PythonScikit-learnOptunaBioinformatics
GitHub

Thinking Like a Botanist: MLLMs with Chain-of-Inquiry

Contributed large-scale data collection for PlantInquiryVQA — 24,964 images, 138,078 QA pairs with reasoning chains. Evaluated SOTA MLLMs (Qwen, Grok, Mistral, Gemma). Published in ACL Findings 2025 as acknowledged contributor.
